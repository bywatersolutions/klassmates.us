What We Do

This service offers free hosted installations of the Koha Open Source ILS to library school instructors for use in their classrooms. Using this service library school students can learn what it means to use a real ILS before entering the workforce.

Who We Support

ByWater Solutions supports libraries using Koha around the globe through our first rate support services and via the Koha Klassmates program. Contact us for more information on how you can join them.
